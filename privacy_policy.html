Privacy Policy for FaceOne

Welcome to FaceOne! We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our property marketing CRM application, FaceOne, which is provided to companies on a subscription basis to manage their property leads.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, and other contact details provided by your company for the purpose of managing property leads and communications with potential buyers or renters.
  • Account Information: When your company subscribes to FaceOne, we collect information related to the subscription, including company name, contact information, and payment details.
  • Property Information: Details about the properties listed and managed through the FaceOne app, including location, pricing, specifications, and other relevant data.
  • Usage Data: We collect data on how the app is accessed and used, including interactions with app features, log data, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide Services: We use the collected data to provide and maintain the FaceOne app, including managing property leads, updating property information, and facilitating communication between companies and potential clients.
  • To Improve Our Services: We analyze usage data to improve the functionality, user experience, and security of the FaceOne app.
  • To Communicate: We may use your contact information to send important updates, marketing communications, or respond to inquiries.
  • For Legal Compliance: We may process your data to comply with legal obligations or in response to lawful requests by public authorities.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • With Your Consent: We may share your information with third parties if you have given your consent to do so.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the FaceOne app, such as cloud storage providers, payment processors, and analytics services. These providers are obligated to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal processes.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Data Rights

  • Access and Correction: You have the right to access and correct your personal data. If you need assistance, please contact us.
  • Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data. However, certain data may be retained for legal or contractual obligations.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails or contacting us directly.

6. Third-Party Links

The FaceOne app may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy within the app or through other communication methods.
